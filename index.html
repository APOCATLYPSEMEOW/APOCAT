What is APOCAT?

APOCAT is a fixed-supply ERC-20 meme token created as a community-driven collectible asset on the Ethereum blockchain. It has no mint function, no ownership controls, and a permanently limited supply of 690,420 tokens, with over 50% sent to a burn address.

Why APOCAT?

APOCAT is just a cat in the apocalypse - a fun meme token for the community. While it serves primarily as a collectible digital asset and community token, our mission is simple: grow and find fellow survivors in the crypto apocalypse.

What Makes It Special?

The token operates on Ethereum Mainnet as a standard ERC-20 token, ensuring compatibility with all major wallets and decentralized exchanges. Its fixed supply and burned tokens create a deflationary model, with no possibility of additional tokens being created.
